# Robes

Three robe families with 5 tiers each. Robes occupy the robe equipment slot.
Robes are the defensive/utility slot — they provide HP, mana, and in one case, offensive
stats.

---

## Mantle

HP-focused robe. High health, some mana at higher tiers. The close-range option.

## Vesture

provides skill and speed. Lower values than specialist robes in any single stat.
