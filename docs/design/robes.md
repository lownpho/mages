# Robes

Three robe families with 5 tiers each. Robes occupy the robe equipment slot.
Robes are the defensive/utility slot — they provide HP, mana, and in one case, offensive stats.

---

## Mantle

HP-focused robe. High health, some mana at higher tiers. The tank option.

| Tier | +HP | +Mana | +Skill | +Speed |
|------|-----|-------|--------|--------|
| Mantle | 10 | 0 | 0 | 0 |
| Better Mantle | 20 | 10 | 0 | 0 |
| Even Better Mantle | 40 | 15 | 0 | 0 |
| Fancy Mantle | 80 | 25 | 0 | 0 |
| Ultimate Mantle | 130 | 50 | 0 | 0 |

---

## Robe

Mana-focused robe. High mana, some HP at higher tiers. The caster option.

| Tier | +HP | +Mana | +Skill | +Speed |
|------|-----|-------|--------|--------|
| Robe | 0 | 10 | 0 | 0 |
| Better Robe | 10 | 20 | 0 | 0 |
| Even Better Robe | 15 | 40 | 0 | 0 |
| Fancy Robe | 25 | 80 | 0 | 0 |
| Ultimate Robe | 50 | 130 | 0 | 0 |

---

## Vesture

Balanced robe. Gives all four stats. The generalist option, and the only robe that provides
skill and speed.

| Tier | +HP | +Mana | +Skill | +Speed |
|------|-----|-------|--------|--------|
| Vesture | 5 | 5 | 0 | 0 |
| Better Vesture | 10 | 10 | 5 | 5 |
| Even Better Vesture | 15 | 15 | 10 | 7 |
| Fancy Vesture | 30 | 30 | 20 | 10 |
| Ultimate Vesture | 50 | 50 | 40 | 15 |
